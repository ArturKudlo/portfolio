Completed:

January, 2019

Description:

The Battle begins, Player and Monster have 100hp or more each. Modal dialog appears and the Player chooses a spell first. It's up to you how to name and animate the spell. Spell types can also be different - with damage or heal effect or both. The Player goes to the Task screen. If the Player solves the task correctly, the spell will be applied, if the answer is wrong, it's Monster's turn to attack. The Monster rolls attack and damages the Player. If the Player wins, there will be a new battle with the next Monster. If the Player loses, high score table appears on the screen and it shows number of defeated Monsters and Player name.Target audience: children 6-8 y.o.

Product Features:
1. The player goes first and selects the method of attack.
2. If you answer correctly, you will do zombie damage or heal yourself.
3. If the answer is not correct, then your lives will decrease.
4. If the player does not answer correctly, then he takes damage.
5. If you defeat a zombie, a new one will appear with more force.
6. If in the mathematical task you encounter a fractional number, then feel free to round it to an integer number ..

Technology used:

HTML5, CSS3, Javascript
